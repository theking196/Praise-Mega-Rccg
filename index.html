About Our Church

Welcome to RCCG Praise Sanctuary, a vibrant community of believers located in the heart of Ikangba Housing Estate, Ijebu-Ode. We are part of the global Redeemed Christian Church of God family, committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and building strong, faith-centered relationships.

Our church is a place where people from all walks of life come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith. We believe in the power of prayer, the importance of biblical teaching, and the transformative love of Jesus Christ. Whether you're seeking spiritual guidance, looking for a church family, or simply want to experience God's presence, you'll find a warm welcome here.

Join us as we continue to serve our community and spread the message of hope, love, and redemption to all who need it.
